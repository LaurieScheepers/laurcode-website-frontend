For big apps I like to bill per milestone. These milestones can be as basic as initial mockups and prototypes and alpha, beta and production releases. But this can be easily changed per the client's wishes.

For refactoring, maintenance, and bug-fixing, I charge per hour. Contact me so that we can figure out together what my per-hour billing rate would be.

For stealth projects, R&D, MVP's and Proof Of Concepts, etc, I prefer to charge per project, as these things are difficult to base on either milestones or per-hour work as it's more of an exploration into technology for which I can't predict how long it would take to finish.

I am also keen for code reviews and upskilling of any employees, for this I also charge per hour.
