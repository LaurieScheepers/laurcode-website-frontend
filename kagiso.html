After Reach and a bit of a break I started working for Kagiso Io - the digital side of Kagiso Media.

This was an exciting shift for me! Immediately I took over 4 streaming apps, and quite popular ones at that! East Coast Radio (4.6 rating), JacarandaFM (4.2 rating), Barney Simon's Rockon Radio (4.7 rating) and Soundbar (4.4 rating).

In all these apps I did refactoring, maintenance and new features.

I was also the sole Android developer on a new app, LiveAmp (which is still in beta).

Kagiso IO is a great company, and I expect them to still deliver a lot of great apps in the future.
