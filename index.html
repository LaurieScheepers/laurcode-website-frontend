Making rich, creative mobile apps that fulfill the needs of users

Welcome, my name is Laurie Scheepers. I am an experienced developer and designer specializing in mobile apps. I'm up for exploring any idea that closes a "gap" and always take special care to create a user-friendly, experience-rich app that is a joy to use.

For info on how I, as a freelancer, can work together with you as a client, open the menu on the right and check out "How I work". If you like what you read, feel free to contact me using the form at the bottom of the page.

For me the following makes a great app:

  • A good code-base
  • A unique UI (that's including use of colors and visually-pleasing animations)
  • Intuitive user-experience
  • Seamless functionality

So if we can work together to achieve that, I will be happy!
