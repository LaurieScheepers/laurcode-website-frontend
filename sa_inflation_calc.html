One day my dad and I had a conversation about financial things and he told me he used to always calculate the inflation-adjusted present value of past-value Rand currency in his head.

I figured why not create a very simple app to quickly and easily calculate the inflation-based present value without needing any mental calculation? So I built it over a weekend and released to the Play Store and today ~77 people around the world still use it on an occasional basis, of which I'm quite proud actually (I know it's not a lot, but this is without any promotion at all).
